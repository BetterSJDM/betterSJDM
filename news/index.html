News & Updates

Stay informed about the latest happenings in San Jose del Monte City

Leadership

Mayor Robes' 2026 Message: "Service Over Resolutions"

January 1, 2026

Mayor Florida "Rida" Robes announced she is skipping traditional New Year's resolutions for 2026. Instead, she emphasized a "continued commitment" to serving the people of SJDM with leadership rooted in heart and integrity, carrying the theme: "Ngayong 2026, tayo ay aARangkada pa rin tungo sa tuloy-tuloy na progreso!"

Source: Politiko Central Luzon

Safety

New Year's Safety: Stray Bullet Incident

January 1, 2026

While Central Luzon was generally peaceful, the Philippine National Police (PNP) reported a stray bullet incident in San Jose del Monte during the New Year revelry. This was one of the few isolated safety incidents in the region amidst otherwise strict firecracker regulations.

Source: Philippine Star

Infrastructure

MRT-7 Station Relocation Confirmed

December 2025

The Department of Transportation (DOTr) and the SJDM local government have finalized the relocation of the MRT-7 SJDM Station. It has been moved from the original Muzon-Tungkong Mangga intersection plan to a location closer to the SJDM-North Caloocan boundary to better manage traffic and serve commuters.

Source: Philippine Information Agency

Youth & Sports

First Intertown Esports Tournament

December 2025

The city solidified its support for esports by hosting the first-ever "Intertown Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Tournament" at Starmall SJDM. Spearheaded by the City Youth and Sports Development Office (CYSDO), recognizing esports as a key youth development platform.

Source: City Government of San Jose del Monte

Development

Infrastructure Status: Government Center & River Esplanade

December 2025

The administration continues its push for the "highly urbanized city" vision with ongoing works at the New Government Center. While the River Esplanade project has faced some contract adjustments, the broader development of the People's Park facilities remains a priority for city tourism.

Source: CSJDM Comprehensive Development Plan 2023-2028 (PDF)

Utilities

Termination of PrimeWater Joint Venture

November 2025

In a major utility shake-up, the City Government of SJDM formally terminated its Joint Venture Agreement (JVA) with PrimeWater Infrastructure Corp. Mayor Robes cited poor service and erratic water supply as the primary reasons. The city has since activated "Water Task Force Tugon" to manage the transition.

Source: The Manila Times

Education

BulSU Sarmiento Campus Infrastructure Upgrade

November 2025

Bulacan State University (BulSU) Sarmiento Campus inaugurated several completed infrastructure projects. These upgrades are part of the university's effort to improve facilities for the growing student population in SJDM, ensuring better academic environments for the 2026 school year.

Source: Bulacan State University
