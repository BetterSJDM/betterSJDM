Agriculture

Agriculture Services

Support for farmers and agricultural development

Dispersal of Assorted Seeds and Seedlings

Planting materials for backyard, urban, and communal gardening needs (residents, barangays, schools, HOAs, institutions)

Fee: Free Time: 7 minutes
Requirements:
  • Physical presence of client
  • Letter Request (if beyond walk-in maximum limit)
  • Matrix Forms/Client Feedback Form

Distribution of Agricultural Inputs

Rice production inputs (certified/hybrid seeds, inorganic fertilizers) and fruiting bags for mushroom production

Fee: Free Time: 3 minutes per farmer
For masterlisted rice farmers & mushroom growers:
  • General Masterlist of Farmers/RSBSA
  • List of Participants during Production Training
  • Government Issued ID (Original & Photocopy)

Provision of Technical Assistance

Basic agricultural services: soil analysis, pest & disease surveillance, damage assessment from calamity

Fee: Free Time: Varies
For farmers and backyard gardeners:
  • Request Form (pro forma)
  • Pictures of damaged commodity (for verification)

Tractor Custom Servicing

Farm equipment servicing for agricultural activities

Fee: Free Time: By schedule
Contact City Agriculture Office for scheduling

Responsible Offices

City Agriculture Office

Seeds/seedlings dispersal, agricultural inputs distribution, technical assistance, tractor servicing
