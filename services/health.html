Health Services

Medical consultations, vaccinations, and health programs

City Health Office (CHO) Services

Contact: 044-919-7370 Local 1119 | Hotline: 0956 986 9417 | Email: chocsjdmbulacan@gmail.com

Expanded Program on Immunization

Free vaccines for childhood diseases (0-12 months old)

Fee: Free Time: 46 minutes
Schedule: Wednesday, 8:00 AM - 12:00 NN
  • Physical presence of patient
  • Medical record from Barangay Health Station

Health Certificate

For employment, food handlers, and business requirements

Fee: ₱50 Time: 52 minutes
Requirements:
  • Chest X-ray Result (DOH Accredited)
  • Urinalysis
  • Fecalysis
  • Official Receipt from Treasury

Dental Health Services

Oral health care, tooth extraction, and dental advice

Fee: Free Time: 1 hr 16 mins
Schedule: Mon-Fri, 8:00 AM - 12:00 NN (by appointment)
  • Physical presence of patient
  • Dental record from Barangay Health Station

Maternal Care

Prenatal and postnatal health services for mothers

Fee: Free Time: By appointment
Services include:
  • Prenatal checkups
  • Iron and folic acid supplements
  • Tetanus toxoid vaccination
  • Health education and counseling

Medical Services

General consultation and treatment at City Health Centers

Fee: Free Time: Walk-in
Available at 15 City Health Centers:
  • Vital signs and initial assessment
  • Doctor consultation
  • Medicine dispensing (if available)
  • Referral to hospital if needed

Exhumation/Transfer Permit

Permit for exhumation or transfer of remains

Fee: ₱100-200 Time: 52 minutes
Requirements:
  • Registered Death Certificate (PSA/LCR)
  • Official Receipt from Treasury

City Health Office

San Jose del Monte City Health Office

The City Health Office provides comprehensive primary healthcare services to all residents of San Jose del Monte City. Services listed in the 2025 Citizen's Charter:

Antiretroviral Therapy (ART) Treatment Dental Health Services Environmental Health and Sanitation Expanded Program of Immunization Family Planning HIV Testing Maternal Care Medical Certificate Medical Services Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation
