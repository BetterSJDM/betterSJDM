Public Safety

Public Safety & Emergency Services

POSO Security, CDRRMO Training & Ambulance, Traffic Management, and Tricycle Safety Inspection (External G2C/G2G Services)

Charter Services

Public Safety Services (2025)

Public Order & Safety Office (POSO), City Disaster Risk Reduction & Management Office (CDRRMO), City Traffic Management, and Tricycle Regulatory Unit services from the Citizen's Charter 2025.

Security Services

Deployment of POSO personnel for ensuring safety and security at events. Submit request letter with event details.

Classification: Simple Fee: FREE Processing Time: ~70 minutes (deployment) Office: Public Order & Safety Office

Capacity Building Training/SIMEX/Drills

Disaster preparedness training, simulation exercises (SIMEX), and emergency drills for residents, communities, and institutions.

Classification: External/Internal Fee: FREE Office: CDRRMO Contact: cdrrmo.csjdm@yahoo.com

Ambulance Service (Pre-Hospital Care) EMS

24/7 emergency ambulance service for pre-hospital care, emergency response, and medical transport. Call CDRRMO hotlines.

Classification: External Service Fee: FREE Response Time: ~10 minutes (varies by location) Office: CDRRMO (4C Division, Operations Division) Hotline: 0936 600 0119, 0955 206 7200

Certificate of Training

Official certificate for individuals or organizations who completed CDRRMO training programs, SIMEX, or drills.

Classification: External Service Fee: FREE Office: CDRRMO Training Division

Certificate of Damage Assessment

Assessment certificate for property damage caused by natural disasters or emergencies. Required for financial assistance and insurance claims.

Classification: External Service Fee: FREE Office: CDRRMO Planning Division

Certificate of Hazard Susceptibility

Certificate indicating hazard susceptibility level of a location. Assesses risks from floods, landslides, earthquakes, and other hazards. Required for building and business permits.

Classification: External Service Fee: FREE Office: CDRRMO Research Division

Redemption of Driver's License

Redemption of confiscated driver's license after traffic violation. Present original TVR, pay fines, then redeem license.

Classification: Simple Fee: ₱500 - ₱3,000 (per violation) Processing Time: 8 minutes Office: City Traffic Management & Sidewalk Clearing Operations Group (CTM-SCOG) Personnel: Jhoma Rose P. Magallanes (Ticket Checker I)

Redemption of Impounded Vehicles

Redemption of impounded vehicle after traffic violation. Present TVR, pay fines and impound fees, then redeem vehicle with LTO documents and valid ID.

Classification: Simple Fee: ₱500 - ₱3,000 + impound fees Processing Time: 15 minutes Office: City Traffic Management - CTM-SCOG + Impounding Area Personnel: Jhoma Rose P. Magallanes (Ticket Checker I), Marshall (Impounding Area)

Annual Safety Inspection of Franchised Tricycles

Physical inspection of tricycle-for-hire to ensure road worthiness, safety standards compliance, and passenger safety. Submit request letter to TRU.

Classification: Simple Fee: FREE Processing Time: 6 hours 5 minutes (batch inspection) Office: Tricycle Regulatory Unit (TRU) Personnel: Joselito Ang (Admin Aide IV), Alberto Delos Reyes (Ticket Checker)
