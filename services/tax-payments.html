Taxation

Tax & Payments

Property tax, fees, and payment services

City Treasurer's Office Services

Contact: 044-919-7370 Local 1116-17 | Email: cto_sjdm@yahoo.com.ph

Real Property Tax Statement

Get a statement of your real property tax due before payment

Fee: Free Time: 5 minutes
Requirements (any of these):
  • Valid ID (original or photocopy)
  • Latest Tax Receipt
  • Latest Tax Declaration (City Assessor's Office)
  • Copy of TCT (Registry of Deeds)
  • SPA/Authorization/Secretary's Certificate for representatives

Real Property Tax Payment

Annual tax payment for land and buildings (amilyar)

Fee: Based on assessment Time: 5 minutes
Legal Basis (LGC Section 233, Local Revenue Code):
  • Basic RPT: Assessed Value × 1.1%
  • SEF: Assessed Value × 1%
  • Late payment: 2% interest/month (max 36 months)
Requirement: Statement of Real Property Tax (RPT Division)

Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

Required for oath of office, licenses, permits, receiving salary, and official business

Fee: ₱5 basic + ₱1/₱1,000 income (max ₱5,000) Time: 5 minutes
Requirements:
  • Accomplished Information Sheet (Cash Division)
  • Valid government-issued ID
For Corporations: ₱500 basic + ₱2/₱5,000 receipts (max ₱10,000)
Legal Basis: Local Revenue Code Article 13, Sections 107-113

Tax Clearance

For transfer of ownership, sale, bank loan, GSIS/Pag-ibig requirements

Fee: ₱50 per RPU Time: 15 minutes
Requirements (Original + 1 Photocopy):
  • Real Property Tax Receipt (CTO)
  • Latest Tax Declaration (City Assessor)
  • Valid ID (buyer & seller/requesting party)
  • SPA/Letter of authority with ID (for representatives)
  • Notarized Deed of Sale/Donation/Extra Judicial Settlement
  • Community Tax Certificate (for those without valid ID)
  • Tax Clearance Receipt
Legal Basis: Local Revenue Code Section 238 B8

Real Property Transfer Tax

Tax on transfer of property ownership to another person

Fee: 0.5% of selling price/FMV (whichever is higher) Time: 10 minutes
Requirements (Original + 1 Photocopy):
  • Transfer Certificate of Title (Registry of Deeds)
  • Notarized Deed of Sale/Donation/Partition/Assignment/Consolidation
  • Tax Declaration - Land (City Assessor)
  • Tax Declaration - Building if applicable (City Assessor)
  • Certificate of No Improvement if no building (City Assessor)
  • Tax Clearance (RPT Division, City Treasurer)
Legal Basis: Local Revenue Code Section 76 (Tax Rate 50% of 1%)
Late payment: 25% surcharge + 2%/month interest (max 72%)

Online Tax Collection

Pay via Landbank LinkBiz Portal, GCash, or Maya

Fee: Based on billing Time: 2.75 working days
Requirements: Previous tax receipt, Latest Tax Declaration, TCT, SPA (if applicable), Valid ID
Steps:
  1. Request billing via email with requirements (30 min)
  2. Receive computation via email
  3. Pay through Landbank LinkBiz/GCash/Maya (within 2.5 working days)
  4. Send payment screenshot via email (5 min)
  5. Receive Official Receipt (AF-56) via email (10 min)
Legal Basis: SP Resolutions 2021-1310-11, 2022-072-08
For complaints: Ms. Alena Ric H. Bacuetes - 0960-414-3553

Business Tax Payment

Business Tax

Quarterly or annual business tax payments for registered businesses

Fee: Based on gross receipts Time: Same day
Payment Schedule:
  • Annual: January (with discount)
  • Quarterly: Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct
Payment at: City Treasurer's Office, City Hall

Payment Channels

  • Walk-in at City Treasurer's Office
  • GCash / Maya
  • Landbank LinkBiz Portal

Office Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Location: City Hall, San Jose del Monte City, Bulacan

Responsible Offices

City Treasurer's Office

Tax collection, cedula, clearances, and payment services

City Assessor's Office

Property declaration, tax assessment, and land records

City Budget Office

Obligation requests, barangay budget review, and SEF budget preparation

City Accounting Office

Pre-audit of disbursements, payroll, check issuance, and financial reporting
