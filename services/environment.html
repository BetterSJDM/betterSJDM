Environment

City Environment & Natural Resources Office (CENRO)

Environmental Inspection Clearance and Tree Cutting Permits (External G2B/G2C/G2G Services)

Charter Services

CENRO Services (2025)

City Environment & Natural Resources Office regulatory services from the Citizen's Charter 2025.

Environmental Inspection Clearance

Certificate of Application for Environmental Inspection required for all new and existing commercial establishments applying for business permits. Ensures compliance with Clean Water Act, Clean Air Act, Ecological Solid Waste Management Act, and other environmental laws.

Classification: Simple Type: G2B (Government to Business) Fee: Varies (SWM Fee + Environmental Inspection Fee) Processing Time: 16 minutes Office: CENRO

Certificate of No Objection for Tree Cutting

Certificate required prior to land utilization, road widening, and similar purposes. For general public including contractors of public works. Includes site inspection and barangay/owner clearances.

Classification: Complex Type: G2G/G2C/G2B Fee: ₱300 per tree Processing Time: 3 days 57 minutes Office: CENRO

  • SWM Fee (Schools/Banks): No. of personnel × ₱2.85
  • SWM Fee (Markets): No. of personnel × ₱96.90

Key Environmental Services and Initiatives

01

Drainage & Sewerage Management

Developing a comprehensive system plan to mitigate flooding and protect public health.

02

Solid Waste Management

Implementing the 10-Year Plan including waste collection, segregation, and environmental protection.

03

Flood Control & Mitigation

Declogging drainage, clearing waterways, and rehabilitating canals with CDRRMO collaboration.

Forest & Land Use Programs

Tree planting and reforestation programs to establish forest cover and preserve biodiversity.
