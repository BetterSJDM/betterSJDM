Education

City College of San Jose del Monte (CCSJDM)

Admission, UNIFAST scholarship entrance examination, clinic visit, library services, and certifications (External G2C Services)

Service 1: Admission Procedures

Open to all SJDM residents (preferably). Submit letter of intent, take entrance exam, interview with Deans/Program Chairs, fill out Student Profile Sheet.

Classification: Simple Fee: FREE Time: 45 minutes

Service 2: UNIFAST Scholarship Entrance Exam

Measures intelligence and ability to identify academically able students for UNIFAST scholarship award. Bring 2 valid IDs, ballpen/pencil.

Classification: Simple Fee: FREE Time: 1 hr 33 mins

Service 3: Clinic Visit

Face-to-face health professional encounter for students, faculty, and staff with medical needs. Present student/employee ID.

Classification: Simple Fee: FREE Time: 5-180 mins

Service 4: Checking-In (Returning) of Books

Return borrowed books to Academic Resource Center. Pay fines if overdue before returning.

Classification: Simple Fee: Fines if overdue Time: 3 minutes

Service 5: Certifications

Certificate of Good Moral Character, Honorable Dismissal, Statement of Account, Certificate of Enrollment, etc. Fill out request slip, present student ID.

Classification: Simple Fee: ₱100 per cert Time: 10 minutes
