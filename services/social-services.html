Social Services

Social Services

Support programs for vulnerable sectors and communities

City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)

Contact: 044-919-7370 Local 1111-1115 | Email: cswdo.csjdm@gmail.com

AICS (Financial Assistance)

Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situation - for sickness, death, hunger, or calamity victims

Fee: Free Time: 14 minutes
Medical Assistance:
  • Certificate of Indigency (Medical purpose)
  • Medical Certificate / Clinical Abstract
  • Medical Prescription / Hospital Bill
  • Two (2) valid IDs
  • Letter of Intent to City Mayor

Senior Citizen ID

ID for citizens 60 years and above with benefits and privileges

Fee: Free Time: 11 minutes
Requirements:
  • Barangay Certification of Senior Citizen
  • Three (3) pcs 1x1 ID picture
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Cedula
  • Application form (from CSWDO)
  • One (1) Valid ID with birthdate

PWD ID

ID for persons with disabilities with benefits and privileges

Fee: Free Time: 11 minutes
Requirements:
  • Certificate of Residency/Indigency for PWD
  • Four (4) pcs 1x1 ID picture (white background)
  • Medical Certificate with type of disability
  • Valid ID of emergency contact person
  • Application form (from CSWDO)

Solo Parent ID

ID and benefits for solo parents under RA 11861

Fee: Free Time: 7 working days
Requirements vary by situation:
  • Birth Certificate(s) of child/children
  • CENOMAR or Marriage Certificate
  • Sworn Affidavit (sole parental care)
  • Barangay Official Affidavit of residency
  • Solo Parents Orientation Seminar Certificate

Social Case Study Report

Document for seeking assistance from other institutions

Fee: Free Time: 22 minutes
Requirements:
  • Certificate of Indigency from Barangay
  • Medical Certificate (for medical)
  • Hospital Bill (for medical)
  • Death Certificate (for burial)
  • Funeral Contract (for burial)

Burial Assistance

Financial assistance for burial expenses

Fee: Free Time: 14 minutes
Requirements:
  • Certificate of Indigency (Burial purpose)
  • Funeral Contract / Promissory Note
  • Death Certificate
  • Two (2) valid IDs
  • Letter of Intent to City Mayor
