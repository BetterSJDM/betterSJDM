City Treasurer's Office

Tax collection, fee payments, and financial services for San Jose del Monte City residents

Office

City Treasurer

Location

City Hall

Hours

8AM - 5PM

Days

Monday - Friday

Real Property Tax Payment

Annual tax payment for land, buildings, and improvements

Requirements

  • Statement of Real Property Tax (RPT Division)
  • Valid ID of Property Owner or Representative
  • Authorization Letter (if representative)

Payment Details

  • Fee: Based on assessed value (1.1% Basic + 1% SEF)
  • Discount: Early payment discount (if paid in January)
  • Penalty: 2% per month for late payment (max 36 months)
  • Processing: 5 minutes

Per Requirement Annual

Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

Issuance of cedula for individuals and corporations

Individual Cedula

  • Valid ID
  • Proof of Income (if employed)
  • Basic Fee: ₱5.00
  • Additional: ₱1.00 per ₱1,000 income (max ₱5,000 total)
  • Processing: 5 minutes

Corporate Cedula

  • SEC/DTI Registration
  • Business Permit
  • Basic Fee: ₱500.00
  • Additional: ₱2.00 per ₱5,000 receipts (max ₱10,000 total)
  • Processing: 5 minutes
