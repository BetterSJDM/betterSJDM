Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situation (AICS)

Service provided to disadvantaged clients in crisis due to sickness, death, hunger, and victims of calamities

Service Classification

External/Internal Service - For all indigent San Jose□os in Crisis Situation

Requirements by Type

1. MEDICAL ASSISTANCE:

  • Certificate of Indigency from Barangay (Medical purpose)
  • Updated Medical Certificate / Clinical Abstract
  • Updated Medical Prescription / Hospital Bill
  • Two (2) valid IDs
  • Letter of Intent to City Mayor

2. BURIAL ASSISTANCE:

  • Certificate of Indigency from Barangay (Burial purpose)
  • Funeral Contract / Promissory Note
  • Letter of Intent to City Mayor
  • Two (2) valid IDs
  • Death Certificate

3. FINANCIAL / EMERGENCY FOOD ASSISTANCE:

  • Two (2) valid IDs
  • Certificate of Indigency (Financial assistance purpose)
  • Letter of Intent to City Mayor

Issuance of Solo Parent ID

Complex Service - For solo parents under RA 11861 who solely provide parental care and support

Requirements (Vary by Situation)

Common Requirements for All:

  • Birth Certificate(s) of dependent child/children
  • Sworn Affidavit of Solo Parent Status
  • Barangay Attestation
  • Solo Parents Orientation Seminar Certificate

Additional Requirements by Specific Situation:

  • Rape (Sec 4a1): Complaint/Judicial Affidavit, Medical Record
  • Death of Spouse (Sec 4a2): Marriage Certificate, Death Certificate
  • Detention/Criminal Conviction (Sec 4a3): Marriage Cert, Certificate of Detention (3+ months)
  • Physical/Mental Incapacity (Sec 4a4): Marriage Cert/Affidavit, Medical Records (not more than 3 months old) or PWD ID
  • Legal/De Facto Separation (Sec 4a5): Marriage Cert, Judicial Decree or Affidavit of 2 disinterested persons
  • Nullity/Annulment/Divorce (Sec 4(1)5): Marriage Cert annotated, Judicial Decree
  • Abandonment (Sec 4a7): Marriage Cert/Affidavit, Affidavit of 2 disinterested persons, Police/Barangay record
  • OFW Spouse/Family (Sec 4b): Marriage Cert/Proof of relationship, POEA-SEC, Passport with stamps or BI cert (12 months), Proof of income
  • Unmarried Parent (Sec 4c): CENOMAR (Certificate of No Marriage)
  • Legal Guardian/Adoptive/Foster Parent (Sec 4d): Proof of Guardianship/Adoption/Foster Care
  • Relative 4th civil degree (Sec 4e): Death cert/Cert of incapacity/Judicial declaration/Police records (6+ months absence), Proof of relationship
  • Pregnant Woman (Sec 4f): Barangay attestation

Service Not Found in Charter

Women's Welfare is not listed as a standalone service in the SJDM Citizen's Charter 2025

Important Information

While CSWDO provides various programs and assistance for women, "Women's Welfare" is not listed as a specific standalone service in the official Charter.

Women in crisis situations may be assisted through:

  • AICS (Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situation) - For medical, burial, or financial assistance
  • Social Case Study Report - For documentation and referral to other institutions
  • Solo Parent ID - For solo mothers who qualify under RA 11861

For specific assistance:

  • Contact CSWDO: 044-919-7370 Local 1111-1115
  • Email: cswdo.csjdm@gmail.com
  • Visit: CSWDO Office, City Hall

Service Not Found in Charter

Children's Welfare is not listed as a standalone service in the SJDM Citizen's Charter 2025

Important Information

While CSWDO provides various programs and assistance for children, "Children's Welfare" is not listed as a specific standalone service in the official Charter.

Children in crisis situations may be assisted through:

  • AICS (Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situation) - For medical, burial, or emergency assistance for children and families
  • Social Case Study Report - For documentation and referral to DSWD or other institutions for child welfare concerns

For child protection and welfare concerns:

  • Contact CSWDO: 044-919-7370 Local 1111-1115
  • Email: cswdo.csjdm@gmail.com
  • Visit: CSWDO Office, City Hall
  • For emergencies: Contact PNP or DSWD directly
