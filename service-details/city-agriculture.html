City Agriculture Office Services

Official services from SJDM Citizen's Charter 2025

Dispersal of Assorted Seeds and Seedlings

Provision of planting materials to individuals, barangays, schools, HOAs, institutions for backyard, urban, communal gardening needs

Requirements

  • Physical presence of client
  • Letter Request (if beyond walk-in maximum limit)
  • Matrix Forms/Client Feedback Form

Service Details

  • Classification: Simple, G2C/G2G
  • Who may avail: Bonafide residents
  • Section: City Plant Nursery
  • Personnel: Officer of the day, Farm Worker II

Fee

Free of charge

₱0.00

Processing Time

7 minutes total (fill form → check → prepare → distribute → file)

7 Minutes

Procedure

4 steps: Client fills matrix form (1-2min) → Officer checks (1min) → Prepare materials (2min) → Distribute & feedback (2min)

4 Steps

Distribution of Agricultural Inputs

Distribution of agricultural inputs for rice production (certified/hybrid seeds, inorganic fertilizers) and fruiting bags for mushroom production conducted every start of cropping season and after production trainings

Requirements

  • General Masterlist of Farmers/RSBSA
  • List of Participants during Production Training
  • Government Issued ID (Original & Photocopy)

Service Details

  • Classification: Simple, G2C
  • Who may avail: Masterlisted Rice Farmers & Mushroom Growers ONLY
  • Section: Extension Services Section
  • Personnel: AEW, Mushroom Coordinator, Property Custodian

Fee

Free of charge

₱0.00

Processing Time

15 minutes per farmer total

15 Minutes

Procedure

Present ID & sign (3min) → Verify list (2min) → Fill feedback (2min) → Issue gate pass (3min) → Receive inputs (3min) → File (2min)

5 Steps

Provision of Technical Assistance

Conduct of technical assistance for basic agricultural services: soil analysis, pest & disease surveillance & identification, damage assessment caused by calamity, and other simple related agricultural concerns. Reported through text messages, phone calls, and walk-in visits

Requirements

  • Request Form (pro forma)
  • Pictures of damaged commodity for verification purposes

Service Details

  • Classification: Simple, G2C/G2G
  • Who may avail: Farmers and Backyard Gardeners
  • Section: Extension Services Section
  • Personnel: AEW (Agricultural Technologist), Officer of the day

Fee

Free of charge

₱0.00

Processing Time

5 minutes to 1 day 1 hour 13 minutes (varies by concern)

Varies

Procedure

Fill request form (2min) → Interview (5-10min) → Field visit (1 day) → Photo documentation (1hr) → Explain findings (5min) → Sign form (1min)

6 Steps
