Service 3: Zoning Certification

For land use verification and business compliance (Simple - G2C/G2G)

Requirements (Land Use)

  • Proof of Ownership (TCT/Tax Declaration/Tax Receipt - photocopy & original for verification)
  • Vicinity Map and Lot Plan (prepared and signed by private Geodetic/Civil Engineer)
  • Barangay Clearance and CTC (Community Tax Certificate)
  • Notarized Special Power of Attorney (if representative)

Requirements (Business)

  • Proof of Ownership (TCT/Tax Declaration/Tax Receipt)
  • DTI approved business name
  • Lease Contract (if renting)
  • Barangay Clearance

Details

  • Classification: Simple
  • Fee: Based on Zoning Ordinance (Residential: ₱240 + 0.5% of project cost excess of ₱100k, Commercial: ₱500 + 0.5% excess, Institutional: ₱300 + 0.5% excess)
  • Penalty: ₱800 residential, ₱1,300 commercial/institutional (without permit)
  • Processing: 2 days
  • Who can apply: Residential, commercial, industrial, institutional developments

Service 1: Locational Clearance for Simple/Non-Critical Projects

For residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional development prior to Building/Business Permit (Simple - G2C/G2G)

Requirements

  • Proof of Ownership (1 photocopy & original for verification):
    • If owner: TCT, Tax Declaration, Tax Receipt
    • If NHA/CMP: NHA Certification, UPAO Certification, Contract to Sell
    • If from developer: Copy of TCT with Tax Dec/Receipt, Deed of Sale
  • Building Plan: Floor area >40sqm (Site Development Plan, Architectural Plan, Bill of Materials); Floor area <40sqm (Sketch Plan only)
  • Barangay Clearance and CTC (Community Tax Certificate)
  • Notarized Special Power of Attorney (if representative)
  • Photo documents (actual project status)
  • Note: For existing projects (AS BUILT PLAN) and projects along major roads, Traffic Clearance required

Details

  • Classification: Simple
  • Fee: Residential: ₱240 + (50% of 1% of project cost excess of ₱100,000); Commercial: ₱500 + (50% of 1% excess)
  • Penalty: ₱800 residential, ₱1,300 commercial (constructing without permit)
  • Processing: 3 days total
  • Who can apply: New construction, renovation, extension projects

Service 2: Locational Clearance for Complex/Critical Projects

For critical projects requiring ocular inspection and evaluation (Complex - G2C/G2G)

Requirements

  • All requirements from Simple/Non-Critical Projects (Service 1)
  • Additional Requirements for Critical Projects (Industrial, institutional, large commercial, piggery/poultry farm, markets, funeral services):
    • Environmental Compliance Certificate (ECC) or Certificate of Non-Coverage (CNC) from EMB-DENR
    • Barangay Council Resolution
    • Written Conformity/Certificate of No-Objection from neighbors/adjacent property owners
    • HOA Resolution/Certificate of No-Objection (for commercial/institutional inside subdivision)
    • Affidavit of Undertaking/Sinumpaang Salaysay
    • Traffic Impact Assessment Plan/Traffic Management Plan
    • Evaluation Report of proposed project

Details

  • Classification: Complex
  • Fee: Residential: ₱240 + (50% of 1% excess of ₱100k); Commercial: ₱500 + (50% of 1% excess); Institutional: ₱300 + (50% of 1% excess)
  • Penalty: ₱800 residential, ₱1,300 commercial/institutional (without permit)
  • Processing: 6 days 27 minutes (includes ocular inspection, evaluation report, City Mayor preliminary approval)
  • Includes: Ocular inspection (2 mins) + Evaluation report (3 days) + Processing (3 days)

Service 4: Locational Clearance/Development Permit for Highly Technical Projects

For highly technical development projects (Highly Technical - External/Internal Service)

Service Information

  • Classification: Highly Technical
  • Type: External/Internal Service - G2C/G2G
  • Requirements: Contact CPDO for specific requirements based on project type and complexity
  • Processing Time: Varies depending on project complexity and technical evaluation required
  • Fees: Based on project cost and zoning ordinance applicable to highly technical developments

General Application Process

Standard steps for CPDO services

Submit Requirements

Fill out application form and submit all required documents for assessment (10 mins)

Assessment & Payment

Receive assessment fee and order of payment, pay at City Treasury Office (5 mins)

Processing

CPDO processes documents, prints clearance/certification, prepares for approval (2-6 days depending on service)

Approval & Signing

Document checked and signed by Zoning Administrator/City Planning Coordinator (4 mins)

Claim Document

Sign logbook, claim signed document, fill out Client Satisfaction form (5 mins)

For Complex Projects

Includes ocular inspection (2 mins) and evaluation report submission to City Mayor's Office for preliminary approval (adds 3 days)

Important Notes

  • Locational clearance is REQUIRED before applying for building permit
  • Zoning certification is REQUIRED for business permit applications
  • Health protocols must be observed
  • Bring original documents for verification (photocopies will be kept)
  • For projects along major roads or existing AS BUILT PLAN, secure Traffic Clearance from City Traffic Management Office
  • Critical projects (industrial, large commercial, piggery/poultry, markets, funeral services) require additional clearances from DENR-EMB, barangay resolution, neighbor conformity, HOA approval, and traffic impact assessment
  • Complex projects undergo ocular inspection and require City Mayor preliminary approval
