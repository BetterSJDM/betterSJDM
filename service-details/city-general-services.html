Service 1: Repair of Government Property Buildings/Facilities (Major Repair)

Highly Technical - Internal Service (G2G) - For all LGU CSJDM offices including national offices

Requirements

  • Request for Repair and Maintenance Slip (secured from GSO-Administrative Division)
Step Client Steps Agency Action Duration Personnel
1 Submit Request for Repair and Maintenance Slip Receive, check and record all information 5 mins per request Ma. Theresa Bobis
2 - Forward request slip to Construction and Maintenance Foreman (C&M Foreman) 5 mins Ms. Bobis / Mr. Francisco
3 - Upon initial evaluation, issue JOB ORDER Form with instructions to staff for site inspection 10 mins C&M Foreman
4 - Evaluation of the Extent of Work to be done 15 mins Laborer, Electrician, Carpenter, Plumber
5 - Prepare the Job Order Slip 10 mins Laborer, Electrician, Carpenter, Plumber
6 - Prepare SAI/RIS for supplies and materials needed for repair 10 mins per job order Laborer, Electrician, Carpenter, Plumber
7 - Forward SAI/RIS/Request Slip to Storekeeper 5 mins Laborer, Electrician, Carpenter, Plumber
8 - Receive the available supplies to be used 10 mins Laborer, Electrician, Carpenter, Plumber
9 - If all needed materials/supplies are available, proceed to necessary repair as stated in Job Order Major: 5 days
Minor: 2 days
Immediate: 2 hours		 Laborer, Electrician, Carpenter, Plumber

Total Processing Time

  • Major Repair: 5 days 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Minor Repair: 2 days 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Immediate Repair: 3 hours 10 minutes
  • Fee: NONE (FREE - Internal Service)

Service 2: Reproduction of Various Forms and Documents

Simple - G2G Service - For LGU CSJDM offices and national offices in CSJDM

Requirements

  • Duly signed and accomplished Request Form (secured from GSO-Administrative Section)
  • Forms/documents to be reproduced (forwarded by requesting office)
  • Paper/s to be used (provided by requesting office)
Step Client Steps Agency Action Duration Personnel
1 Submit duly signed and accomplished Request Form Receive, review and evaluate the Request Form as well as the documents to be reproduced 2 mins per request Repro Machine Operator III
2 Endorse the type of paper to be used for reproduction Endorse the Request Form to the Head of GSO for approval 5 mins per request Repro Machine Operator III
3 - Reproduce the forms or document 15 mins per ream Machine Operator, GSO

Total Processing Time

  • Total Time: 22 minutes per ream
  • Fee: NONE (FREE - G2G Service)
  • Purpose: To save budget by catering reproduction in-house instead of procuring service from outside sources

Service 3: Requisition and Issuance of Inventory Items

Complex - G2G Service - For LGU CSJDM offices and national offices in CSJDM

Requirements

  • Supplies Availability Inquiry (SAI) - secured from CGSO Supplies Section or Administrative Section
  • Requisition and Issue Slip (RIS) - secured from CGSO Supplies Section or Administrative Section
Step Client Steps Agency Action Duration Personnel
1 Submit duly accomplished SAI (2 copies) and RIS (3 copies) Receive, review and verify SAI and RIS as to completeness of information; record submitted SAI and RIS and forward to Supply Officer II 5 mins per SAI/RIS GSO-Supplies Division Personnel
2 - Upon receipt of SAI and RIS, determine availability/status of stocks from Supply Stock Card 20 mins per RIS Supply Officer II
3 - Verify requested inventory items in RIS and quantity against PPMP submitted by respective offices; Fill up RIS as to Quantity to be issued and Remarks 20 mins per RIS Supply Officer II, Supply Officer I, Admin Aide VI, Admin Aide IV
4 - If items requested are not included in submitted PPMP, inform concerned office for necessary revision of PPMP 5 mins per office Supply Officer II
5 - Prepare the available inventory items 20 mins per RIS Storekeeper, Warehouseman
6 Sign the "Received by" portion of the RIS Issuance of requested supplies 5 mins per office Storekeeper, Warehouseman
7 Get the 3rd copy of RIS Record the signed RIS and forward to assigned personnel for preparation of Report of Supplies and Materials Issued (RSMI) 5 mins per RIS Storekeeper, Warehouseman
8 - Post the issued inventory items on respective Stock Card 20 mins per RIS Supply Officer I, Admin Aide VI, Admin Aide IV
9 - Preparation and Submission of RSMI to City Accounting Office 6 days after posting inventory items to Stock Cards Supply Officer II, Supply Officer I, Admin Aide VI, Admin Aide IV

Total Processing Time

  • Total Time: 6 days, 1 hour & 40 minutes per RIS
  • Fee: NONE (FREE - G2G Service)
  • Purpose: Defines actions and responsibilities from receiving request thru RIS to updating Stock Card and providing RSMI to City Accounting Office for recording

Office Information

  • City General Services Office, City Hall, San Jose del Monte City, Bulacan
  • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Type: Internal Service (G2G - Government to Government)
  • Who may avail: All LGU CSJDM offices and national offices in CSJDM

Important Notes

  • All services are FREE - no fees charged (Internal G2G)
  • Service 1: Property repair includes major (5 days), minor (2 days), and immediate (2 hours) repairs
  • Service 2: Forms reproduction catered in-house to save budget instead of outsourcing
  • Service 3: Inventory requisition requires SAI and RIS forms from GSO Supplies Section
  • All requests must be properly documented with required forms from GSO-Administrative Division or Supplies Section
  • For Service 3, requested items must be included in submitted PPMP (Project Procurement Management Plan)
