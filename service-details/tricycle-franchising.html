Process Flow

Step-by-step procedures for tricycle franchise and records request

01

Submit Requirements

Submit accomplished application form and all requirements to Tricycle Regulatory Unit

02

Inspection & Evaluation

TRU accepts, evaluates documents and inspects tricycle unit (10 minutes)

03

Tax Assessment & Payment

TRU encodes data, assesses tax (₱1,287.50), issues Tax Order of Payment. Pay at cashier (15 minutes)

04

Review & Approval

Present Official Receipt. TRU reviews, approves and prepares MTOP

05

Release MTOP

Receive your Motorized Tricycle Operator's Permit with sticker and plate

01

Receiving Letter of Request

VMO/SBO Staff receives the letter of request

02

Approval/Disapproval

Vice Mayor or Secretary to the Sanggunian approves/disapproves the request

03

Review & Determination

Records Officer II reviews and determines document availability

04

Retrieval & Reproduction

Records Staff retrieves and reproduces the requested documents

05

Releasing & Stamping

Records Officer III releases and stamps the requested documents
