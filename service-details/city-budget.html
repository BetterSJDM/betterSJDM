Review and Endorsement of Barangay & SK Budget

The City Budget Office reviews and endorses the approved budget of the component Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of the LGU, including annual and supplemental budgets. Ensures compliance with statutory, contractual obligations and budgetary requirements prior to endorsement.

Important Note

  • Within ten days (10) from the approval of the Barangay Budget, copies of their Annual Budget shall be furnished to the Sangguniang Panlungsod for review through the City Budget Office.
  • Both Annual and Supplemental Barangay & SK budgets are processed through this service.
